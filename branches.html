Our Branches



Crytal Inn Sri Lanka

Our beachfront hotel provides a haven of tropical peace and tranquilly located along the shores of Sri Lanka. Enjoy the breathtaking sunrise views as you awaken to the soothing sound of the waves lapping the shore. You can spend your day relaxing under swaying palm trees, participating in water sports, or just taking in the peace and quiet of the Indian Ocean thanks to the direct access to the pristine beach. Our beachfront hotel is your doorway to an unforgettable island escape.





Crytal Inn Switzerland

Our mountainside hotel, sitting within the stunning Swiss Alps. The hotel provides a comfy place for those looking for relaxation and adventure. The wooden chalet-style architecture blends in with the natural surroundings, and each room offers breathtaking views each season.

Crystal Inn Los Angeles

Our city hotel, situated near the core of Los Angeles, captures the vibrant spirit of this legendary city. Spend the day immersed in the city's vibrant energy before retiring to the comfort of our luxurious accommodations at night. Whether you're visiting Los Angeles on business or for pleasure, our hotel is the perfect place to experience a unforgettable trip.
