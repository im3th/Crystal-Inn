At our BBQ guests can gather to enjoy a variety of grilled meats in the welcoming atmosphere of a hotel setting. We offer expertly prepared meats, including succulent steaks, marinated chicken, and smoky ribs, cooked to absolute perfection on open grills. A hotel BBQ event offers the ideal fusion of flavors and a social setting,served with a variety of delectable sides like fresh salads,grilled vegetables, and savory sauces.




Fine Dining



The Crystal Inn offers first-rate food and outstanding customer service in a refined environment. visitors are given a culinary experience in which every aspect is planned to produce a special and elegant dining experience. This is a fantastic value because it combines high-quality ingredients, expert craftsmanship, and the chance to sample various flavors.
